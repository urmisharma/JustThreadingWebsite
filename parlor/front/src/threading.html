What is Eyebrow Threading?

Eyebrow Threading is a very precise method of shaping the eyebrows using a 100% cotton thread. It is a quick, gentle, and sanitary way of removing unwanted facial hairs. Unlike waxing, the top layers of skin is never burned or irritated, in the process. The thread is rolled along the surface of the skin grabbing and lifting the hair out from the follicle. The process takes less than 5 minutes and the results are slower re-growth and finer hair. Threading is also used on other areas of the face such as the upper and lower lip. It also is an excellent alternative for those with very sensitive skin or for those who are on Retin-A, Accutane, Renova, etc.
